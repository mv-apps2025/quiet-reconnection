# Brand Guidelines: The Next Chapter

## Brand Name
**The Next Chapter**

## Tagline
"Your kids flew. Now it's your turn."

## Keywords

- Freedom
- Transformation
- Rediscovery
- Connection
- Purpose

## Color Palette

- **Primary Accent:** Dusty Blue (#6B8A9A)
- **Background:** Ivory White (#FAFAF8)
- **Text:** Deep Charcoal (#2D2D2D)
- **Border/Muted:** Warm Gray (#E8E4E0)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- **Body:** DM Sans (sans-serif)

## Voice

**The Encouraging Coach:** Empathetic but energizing, possibility-focused, action-oriented.

- **Tone:** Warm, validating, and aspirational. Always acknowledge accomplishments before addressing challenges.
- **Language:** Simple, direct, and free of jargon. Use encouraging and empowering words.
- **Perspective:** Speak as a supportive guide who understands the transition and sees the incredible opportunity ahead.

## Key Messaging Principles

1. **Validation-First:** Always acknowledge what they accomplished as parents before addressing the problem
2. **Aspirational Focus:** Emphasize what's possible, not just what's lost
3. **Permission-Giving:** Give them permission to prioritize themselves
